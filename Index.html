About Us

Name: Serene Bistro Location: Nestled in the heart of Whitefield, Bengaluru, Serene Bistro offers a tranquil escape from the city's hustle and bustle. Our restaurant combines a cozy, welcoming ambiance with a commitment to sustainable practices and farm-to-table dining. Cuisine: Our menu features a variety of dishes inspired by global flavors, with a focus on fresh, seasonal ingredients. From hearty soups and salads to flavorful mains and decadent desserts, there’s something for everyone at Serene Bistro. Atmosphere: At Serene Bistro, you’ll find a soothing environment with rustic wooden décor, soft ambient lighting, and a beautiful outdoor garden seating area. It’s the perfect place for a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a casual meal with friends. Mission: We believe in serving delicious, wholesome food that’s good for you and the planet. Our menu features organic ingredients sourced from local farms, ensuring the freshest flavors and supporting our community.

Menu

  • Organic Garden Salad

    A vibrant mix of fresh organic greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, and radishes, tossed in a light vinaigrette made with olive oil, lemon juice, and a hint of honey.

  • Farmhouse Margherita Pizza

    A classic favorite made with a thin, crispy crust, topped with organic tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves. Baked to perfection in our wood-fired oven.

  • Herb-Crusted Grilled Chicken

    Juicy chicken breast marinated in a blend of herbs and spices, grilled to perfection, and served with a side of roasted vegetables and garlic mashed potatoes.

  • Vegetarian Aster Bloom Pasta

    A delightful dish featuring penne pasta tossed with a creamy sauce made from organic Aster flowers, sautéed mushrooms, spinach, and sun-dried tomatoes. Garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.

  • Lemon Basil Sorbet

    A refreshing dessert made with freshly squeezed lemon juice, basil leaves, and a touch of organic honey. Served as a perfect palate cleanser.
